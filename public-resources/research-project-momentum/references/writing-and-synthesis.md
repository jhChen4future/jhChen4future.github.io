# Writing and Synthesis Operations

Writing is a research operation when it forces claims, evidence, and logic into alignment.

## High-value branches
- Contribution audit
- Claim-evidence matrix
- Causal-chain reconstruction
- Competing-explanation section
- Result-to-theory update
- Limitation as boundary condition
- Outline rebuilt around the research question
- Reviewer-style adversarial read

## Anti-degradation rule
Do not improve prose before checking whether the underlying claim is supported. Do not retain an old outline when the research state has changed.

## Progress artifact
Leave a claim-evidence table, revised outline, argument map, response memo, or rewritten section whose scope matches the evidence.
